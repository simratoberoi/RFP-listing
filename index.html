Supply of XLPE Insulated Unarmoured Power Cables for Electrical Infrastructure Project

RFP-2025-0823-XLPE-01
Open
Organization Metro Energy Solutions Ltd.
Issue Date 04-Nov-2025
Deadline 18-Dec-2025, 17:00 IST
General Information
Department Department of Electrical Engineering
Category Power & Energy / Electrical Infrastructure
Procurement Type Open Tender / RFP
Deadline 18-Dec-2025, 17:00 IST
Description
Metro Energy Solutions Ltd. invites proposals from qualified suppliers for the supply of high-quality XLPE insulated unarmoured power cables to support our upcoming electrical infrastructure project. The required cable must have a copper conductor with a cross-sectional area of 6 sqmm and a voltage rating of 0.6/1 kV. The insulation thickness should be 0.7 mm with an overall outer diameter of 8 mm, ensuring compliance with IEC-60502-1 standards. The conductor resistance must be 0.86 ohm/km, the test voltages should be 56/58 kV, and the cable capacitance should be 3.08 µF/km. The supplier must provide cables that have passed type tests and routine tests, and all materials must meet industry-standard quality and safety requirements. Interested vendors should submit proposals including technical specifications, pricing, delivery timelines, and evidence of compliance with the required standards. The expected delivery timeframe is six weeks from the date of award, and vendors are requested to ensure full compliance with the specifications and mandatory tests listed herein.
